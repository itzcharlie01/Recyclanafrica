What is Mr Bin?

MrBin is a tech-enabled waste collection business set to revolutionize the way we manage plastic waste. In a world grappling with environmental challenges, we are poised to become the "Uber of Waste," offering a convenient and sustainable solution. Through our innovative mobile app, individuals and businesses can request waste pickups, while our technology-driven approach optimizes collection routes and compresses plastic waste into bales at our collection centers. 

Nigeria's top 10 beverage brands consume over 600k MT per annum of PET, and less than 15% of that is recycled. This has resulted in a plastic waste crisis and most of the waste goes to landfills and end up in our ocean. MrBin intends to use technology to drive collection of waste materials from source. Over the next 12 months, we plan to establish 16 collection centres across Nigeria. We plan to recover between 2400 tons to 4800 tons per annum, per collection center. Using state of the art technology, we will drive the collection and recycling volumes of PET in Nigeria to global standards.

Efficient Collection

Each collection center will have 2 of our custom built electronic pickup van. And will have 100 waste pickers assigned to them. The waste pickers shall be given a smart trolley for waste pickup. Waste pickers shall have a minimum daily recovery volume. The pickers shall recover materials from individual homes, HORECA (hotels, restaurants and cafes), dump site vendors. Every material picked up shall be logged and be fully traceable across the entire value chain

Setup Plan

MrBin plans to roll out 16 collection centers over 12 months. There are 2 grades/categories of collection centers. Grade A and Grade B. Grade A centers are centers that can recover 150 tons and above. Grade B centers are centers that recover less than 150 tons. MrBin will setup 13 Grade A centers and 3 Grade B centers. Each Grade A center will have 3 bailing machines, 2 E-vans, 1 electric clamp forklift, 150 smart trolleys Each Grade B center will have 2 bailing machines, 1 E-van, 1 electric clamp forklift, 100 smart trolleys

Roll-out Plan

MrBin plans to roll out 16 collection centers over 12 months. 
• 5 Grade A centers will be rolled-out between months 0-3 (Phase 1) 
• 5 Grade A centers will be rolled out between months 4-6 (Phase 2) 
• 4 Grade A centers and 4 Grade B centers will be rolled out between months 7-12 (Phase 3)
